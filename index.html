Why Choose Entraco BKS

Key Advantages

Every Entraco BKS busduct system is engineered and certified to deliver superior safety, durability, and ease of installation.

Water Proof

Fully sealed resin cast construction — no water ingress, no air gaps. Protection up to IP68 for submersible environments.

Highest Ingress Protection

Rated up to IP68 — the highest protection against dust and water ingress for busduct systems in any environment.

Fire Proof

Epoxy resin insulation is inherently flame retardant, providing critical fire safety in high-rise buildings and data centres.

Corrosion Resistant

Premium electrolytic-grade aluminium enclosures resist corrosion — lightweight and built for long service life.

UV Ray Safe

Outdoor and exposed installations are protected — materials rated safe for continuous UV exposure without degradation.

Low Voltage Drop

Larger conductor cross-sections and optimised contact surfaces minimise resistance for better conductivity and lower losses.

Super Compact

Sandwich and resin cast designs require minimal installation space — ideal where cable trays and conduit cannot fit.

Easy to Install

Single-bolt mono-block assembly joints reduce on-site setup time significantly — no special tools required.

Maintenance Free

Sealed resin cast systems require zero routine maintenance — reducing lifetime operating costs for building owners.
