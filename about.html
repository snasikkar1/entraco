About Entraco BKS

An Indo-Swiss Venture. ISO 9001 certified. Over 30 years engineering busduct solutions for industries worldwide.

Our Story

Built on Engineering

As a leader in advanced bus duct technology, Entraco BKS provides innovative, reliable, and efficient solutions. With over 30 years of experience, we prioritize quality, precision, and customer satisfaction.

Entraco BKS is ISO 9001 certified and complies with international standards including IEC, UL, and CSA. An Indo-Swiss Venture headquartered at our manufacturing facility in the MIDC industrial zone of Nashik, Maharashtra.

We design, engineer, manufacture, and install Low Voltage and Medium Voltage busduct systems for some of India's most demanding projects — from high-rise towers and data centres to metro rail networks, renewable energy plants, and defence facilities.
